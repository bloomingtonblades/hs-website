News

Blades Spirit Wear

Our friends at TIS put together this nifty order form for all your Blades spirit wear needs.

Just go to HollowayUSA.com and find stuff you like. Enter order number, size, color, embroidery preferences on the form. Return the form to Jay Baer by August 31. He’ll place the order, collect your money, and get your stuff to you when it arrives.

Also available, but not on the order form (different vendor) are the cool Blades caps. Dark blue with the varsity “B” on front, and Bloomington Blades in white on the back. These are super comfy. $25 each. They are flex fit and come in S/M and L/XL sizes. If you’d like a hat, please let Jay know and he’ll get those ordered too.

This is the one ordering window for team merchandise, so please don’t miss the August 31 deadline.

Updates

Be sure to check the calendar for updated practices times and other changes. Also players and parents should get the TeamApp mobile app. Once you install it, create an account and then search for “Blades HS Hockey” and join! We’ll use TeamApp for quick messages and game-day updates.

Off-Ice Practice Carpools

Sign up to carpool and share the driving for off-ice practices! These are mostly going to be at Creekbend Vineyard, 7508 N Woodall Rd, Ellettsville . Players with a driver’s license are permitted to drive to practices in the Bloomington area.

Columbus Practice Carpools

Sign up to carpool and share the driving for Columbus practices! Reminder: players are not permitted to drive themselves or anyone else to practices in Columbus (even if they are of legal driving age and licensed).

Did you know…

  • Ice rentals are $200-$250 per hour or more. Seasonal expenses for ice are tens of thousands of dollars.
  • We must pay for officials, league fees, tournament fees, coaching education, uniforms and equipment.
  • We receive NO funding from city, county, or school system sources.
  • Our coaches and board members are all volunteers.
  • Player fees alone do not cover our expenses.
